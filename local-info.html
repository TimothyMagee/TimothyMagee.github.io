Pubs and Restaurants

King's Kitchen

This is the main cafeteria at KCL. Convenient and affordable lunch option. Located on Floor -1 of Bush House South East Wing.

Pilpel Falafel

Organizer recommended falafel joint. A 10 minute walk away in a side alley off Fleet Street (opposite the Ye Olde Cheshire Cheese Pub).

Crown Café Bar

An independent Portuguese run sandwich shop between the Strand building and Waterloo bridge.

Mulberry Bush

Southbank pub with botanically-themed decor. This is where the conference dinner will be held.

Ye Olde Cheshire Cheese

Historic English pub. The current establishment was rebuilt in 1667 after the Great Fire of London.

Lowlander Grand Café

Belgian pub with extensive beer selection. A 10 minute walk along Drury Lane.

The Wellington

Nearby pub at the intersection of Strand and Waterloo Bridge with plenty of outdoor seating to enjoy the famed English weather.

Hotels

Royal National Hotel

Most of the conference speakers will be staying here.
